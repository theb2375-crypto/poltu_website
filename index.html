Questions

Before you ask.

The things people usually want to know before they sign up.

Is PoltuReform affiliated with any political party?+
No. It's an independent civic platform. Candidates from any party — or none — can register, and the feed treats every listing the same way.
How are candidates verified before they appear on the platform?+
Every applicant submits government ID and meets the standard eligibility checks for the office they're seeking before a profile goes live.
Where does my donation actually go?+
Funds sit in escrow until a candidate requests a release. A release needs 50% donor approval, and a bill or receipt has to be submitted afterward as proof.
Can anyone register as a candidate?+
Any citizen who meets the eligibility requirements for the office — age, residency, and so on — can apply, regardless of party backing or personal wealth.
What's the ₹5,000 deposit for?+
It's a refundable filing fee, there to keep registrations serious without making them inaccessible the way real campaign costs do.
When can I actually use the app?+
The design is complete and beta testing is being planned for select cities. Join the waitlist below to get early access.
